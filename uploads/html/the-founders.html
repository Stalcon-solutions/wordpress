Amen Zoabi

As a skilled construction and property executive, Amen has a reputation as a dedicated business leader and industry expert who has been delivering outstanding results for more than two decades.

As a co-founder of Binah, Amen has been integral in projects such as the construction of the award-winning Western Sydney University Liverpool Campus and the rezoning of the Peter Warren superlot in the heart of the Liverpool CBD.

Khalil Hafza

As Managing Director of Binah Khalil is distinguished by his delivery of iconic, award-winning projects that are recognised at both state and national levels. Over 25 years, he has set high standards in quality, safety, and sustainability in growth environments, earning the trust of investors throughout New South Wales and Queensland.

Khalil is deeply committed to community development, exemplified by his co-founding of Unity Grammar School in 2008. His involvement has spurred significant accomplishments within the sector, demonstrating a synergy between his drive for exceptional performance and a profound dedication to supporting community growth.
